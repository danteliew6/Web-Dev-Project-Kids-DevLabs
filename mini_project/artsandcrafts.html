Arts and Crafts

Join us in Family Day 2022 for some arts and crafts session! For this year's arts and crafts workshop, you will learn how to fold origami! Each session is 45 minutes long!

You can choose one of the origamis below to build during our workshops!

  • Pig Origami

    Pig origamis are fun to build and extremely cute! It's also easy to build, perfect for beginners!

  • Shapes Origami

    Build cool shape origamis by combining several origamis together! This might be a little difficult for beginners but extremely fun if you're up for the challenge!

  • Swan Origami

    Swans require a lot of gentle and small folds, which make it the hardest yet nicest origami!
