Fun Fair Games!

List of Fun Fair Games:

  1. Ring Toss

    • With 30 bottles in place, try your luck by landing the rings onto one of the bottles! Land 3 rings and win yourself a grand prize of a giant plushie!

  2. Balloon Pop

    • In each balloon, there is a mystery card indicating the prize. Pop the balloons with your dart to reveal what prize you win!

  3. Bean Bag Toss

    • Three circles are placed at a distance from the thrower. Land one bean bag in at least two of these circles to win a prize!
